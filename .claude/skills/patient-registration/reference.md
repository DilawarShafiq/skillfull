# Patient Registration & Scheduling Skill

## Overview
This skill covers the front-end revenue cycle processes including patient demographics collection, appointment scheduling, and pre-registration workflows for US healthcare providers.

## Key Competencies

### Patient Demographics Collection
- Collect and verify complete patient information (name, DOB, SSN, address, phone, email)
- Capture emergency contact information
- Document primary care physician and referring provider details
- Obtain and verify patient consent forms (HIPAA, treatment consent, financial responsibility)

### Insurance Information Capture
- Primary, secondary, and tertiary insurance details
- Policy holder information (subscriber name, DOB, relationship to patient)
- Group number, policy number, and plan type
- Effective dates and termination dates
- Coordination of Benefits (COB) rules

### Appointment Scheduling Best Practices
- Match appointment types to appropriate time slots and providers
- Implement overbooking strategies based on no-show rates
- Schedule follow-up appointments before patient leaves
- Send appointment reminders (24-48 hours in advance)
- Document cancellation and rescheduling policies

### Pre-Registration Workflows
- Collect demographic and insurance information before visit
- Verify insurance eligibility prior to appointment
- Calculate and communicate patient financial responsibility
- Obtain prior authorizations when required
- Update patient information at each visit

## Common Errors to Avoid
- Incomplete or inaccurate demographic information
- Failure to verify insurance before service
- Not collecting copays at time of service
- Missing signatures on consent forms
- Incorrect patient matching leading to duplicate records

## Regulatory Considerations
- HIPAA Privacy Rule compliance for PHI collection
- Red Flags Rule for identity verification
- State-specific consent requirements
- Medicare Secondary Payer (MSP) questionnaire requirements

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- Registration accuracy rate (target: >98%)
- Pre-registration completion rate
- No-show and cancellation rates
- Time to complete registration
- Patient satisfaction scores

## Integration Points
- Practice Management System (PMS)
- Electronic Health Record (EHR)
- Insurance eligibility verification systems
- Patient portal
- Appointment reminder systems
