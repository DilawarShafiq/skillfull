# Denial Appeal Letter Templates

## Medical Necessity Appeal

```
[Date]

[Payer Name]
[Payer Address]
Appeals Department

RE: Appeal for Claim Denial
    Patient: [Patient Name]
    Member ID: [ID Number]
    Date of Service: [DOS]
    Claim Number: [Claim #]
    Denial Reason: [CARC Code] - Medical Necessity

Dear Appeals Department,

We are writing to formally appeal the denial of the above-referenced claim. The service was medically necessary based on the following clinical justification:

CLINICAL PRESENTATION:
[Describe patient's condition, symptoms, and clinical findings]

MEDICAL NECESSITY RATIONALE:
[Explain why the service was required for diagnosis/treatment]

SUPPORTING DOCUMENTATION:
- [List attached clinical documentation]
- [Relevant test results]
- [Progress notes]

REGULATORY SUPPORT:
[Reference applicable LCD/NCD, clinical guidelines, or peer-reviewed literature]

Based on the above evidence, we respectfully request reconsideration of this denial and full payment for the services rendered.

Sincerely,
[Provider Name/Credentials]
[NPI]
[Contact Information]
```

## Prior Authorization Denial Appeal

```
[Date]

RE: Retrospective Authorization Request / Appeal
    Patient: [Patient Name]
    Member ID: [ID Number]
    Date of Service: [DOS]
    Procedure: [CPT Code] - [Description]

Dear Medical Director,

We are appealing the denial of services for lack of prior authorization. The services were provided on an urgent/emergent basis, or authorization was obtained but not properly documented.

CIRCUMSTANCES:
[Explain why prior authorization was not obtained or was delayed]

CLINICAL URGENCY:
[Describe medical necessity for immediate treatment]

DOCUMENTATION:
- Authorization reference number (if applicable): [#]
- Clinical documentation supporting urgency
- [Additional supporting documents]

We request retrospective authorization and payment for these medically necessary services.

Sincerely,
[Provider Information]
```

## Timely Filing Exception Appeal

```
[Date]

RE: Timely Filing Exception Request
    Claim Number: [Claim #]
    Date of Service: [DOS]
    Original Submission Date: [Date]

Dear Appeals Department,

We are appealing the denial for timely filing (CARC CO-29) and request an exception based on the following:

PROOF OF TIMELY SUBMISSION:
[Attach one or more of the following]
- Clearinghouse transmission report dated [date]
- Payer acknowledgment/claim receipt
- Electronic confirmation number
- Certified mail receipt

EXPLANATION:
[If applicable, explain circumstances that delayed filing]

We have attached documentation proving the original claim was submitted within the contractual filing deadline of [X] days. We request the claim be processed for payment.

Sincerely,
[Provider Information]
```

## Bundling/NCCI Edit Appeal

```
[Date]

RE: Appeal for Bundled/Unbundled Services
    Claim Number: [Claim #]
    Date of Service: [DOS]
    Procedure Codes: [List codes]

Dear Appeals Department,

We are appealing the denial of [CPT code] which was bundled with [CPT code]. These services were distinct and separately identifiable for the following reasons:

DISTINCT SERVICE JUSTIFICATION:
- Different anatomical site: [explain]
- Different session/encounter: [explain]
- Separate lesion/pathology: [explain]
- Distinct purpose: [explain]

MODIFIER JUSTIFICATION:
Modifier -59 (or appropriate modifier) was applied per NCCI guidelines because:
[Explain why modifier indicator allows unbundling]

DOCUMENTATION:
[Reference operative report, procedure notes showing distinct services]

We request separate reimbursement for the distinct services rendered.

Sincerely,
[Provider Information]
```

## Level of Care/Clinical Denial Appeal

```
[Date]

RE: Appeal for Level of Care Determination
    Patient: [Patient Name]
    Admission Date: [Date]
    Discharge Date: [Date]
    Denied Level: [Inpatient/Observation/etc.]

Dear Medical Director,

We are appealing the determination that the patient did not meet criteria for [level of care]. The patient met medical necessity criteria based on:

ADMISSION CRITERIA MET:
- [Severity of illness indicators]
- [Intensity of service required]
- [Risk factors requiring higher level]

CLINICAL COURSE:
[Document treatment provided, patient response, clinical complexity]

CRITERIA REFERENCE:
[Reference InterQual, Milliman, CMS Two-Midnight Rule, or other criteria]

PEER-TO-PEER REQUEST:
We request a peer-to-peer discussion between your medical director and the attending physician, [Name, Specialty].

Sincerely,
[Provider Information]
```
