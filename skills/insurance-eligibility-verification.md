# Insurance Eligibility & Verification Skill

## Overview
This skill covers the verification of patient insurance coverage, benefits, and authorization requirements to ensure clean claims and reduce denials for US healthcare providers.

## Key Competencies

### Real-Time Eligibility Verification
- Verify coverage status (active/inactive/terminated)
- Confirm effective dates and termination dates
- Identify plan type (HMO, PPO, EPO, POS, HDHP)
- Check network status (in-network vs out-of-network)
- Verify patient responsibility (copay, coinsurance, deductible)

### Benefits Investigation
- Determine covered services under the plan
- Identify benefit limitations and exclusions
- Calculate remaining deductible amounts
- Verify out-of-pocket maximum status
- Check lifetime and annual benefit maximums

### Prior Authorization Management
- Identify services requiring prior authorization
- Submit authorization requests with clinical documentation
- Track authorization status and follow up
- Document authorization numbers and validity periods
- Handle urgent/emergent authorization requests

### Payer-Specific Requirements
- Medicare: ABN requirements, LCD/NCD policies
- Medicaid: State-specific requirements, managed care plans
- Commercial: Payer-specific authorization lists
- Workers' Compensation: Employer and carrier verification
- Tricare: DEERS eligibility verification

## Verification Checklist
- [ ] Patient name and DOB match insurance card
- [ ] Policy and group numbers verified
- [ ] Coverage is active as of date of service
- [ ] Provider is in-network (if applicable)
- [ ] Service is covered under plan
- [ ] Prior authorization obtained (if required)
- [ ] Patient financial responsibility calculated
- [ ] COB determined for multiple coverages

## Common Denial Reasons Related to Eligibility
- CO-27: Expenses incurred after coverage terminated
- CO-29: Time limit for filing has expired
- CO-197: Precertification/authorization not obtained
- CO-198: Precertification/notification absent
- CO-199: Revenue code/bill type requires authorization

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- Eligibility verification rate before service
- Authorization denial rate
- Clean claim rate
- Days to obtain authorization
- Verification accuracy rate

## Best Practices
- Verify eligibility 24-48 hours before scheduled appointment
- Re-verify on day of service for any changes
- Document all verification details in patient record
- Use batch eligibility for scheduled appointments
- Implement automated eligibility checking
